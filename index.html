About me

My name is Mehran. I am doing a PhD in Bioengineering at Auckland Bioengineering Institute. My research is about the verification of numrical models of physiological systems. As a result, I am using my knowledge in Mechanical Engineering and my expertise in coding to develop a tool in Python that can automatically read and verify numerical models of human cellular systems. The reason I am doing this is related to my background in Mechanical Engineering. Mechanics is the study of physical laws in real-world applications of moving objects. Human body is also a physical system that must obey the physical laws. Consequently, the laws of Physics and Thermodynamics must govern at each point in our bodies. To wrap up, I will apply the rules of physics and thermodynamics to check the proposed and studied models' compliance to the real world.
